# **App Name**: T.U.E.M.I. System

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Securely register and authenticate users (administrator, conductor, student/staff) with different access levels.
- Real-time Vehicle Monitoring: Track the real-time location of university transport vehicles on a map. The location of the vehicle will be available to admin, the assigned driver, and to subscribed students/staff.
- Route and Vehicle Management: Enable administrators to register and manage routes, vehicles, and drivers. Record the driver's state during each trip and notify in case of any problems detected via generative AI tool
- Route Assignment and Abonado Management: Allow administrators to assign routes to drivers and manage abonados (users with paid subscriptions), including activating and deactivating their abonado status.
- Estimated Time of Arrival (ETA): Provide students with estimated time of arrival (ETA) and status of the transport, including delays or changes in schedule. Suggest alternative transportation arrangements for users encountering transportation disruptions with a generative AI tool.
- Capacity and Alert Management: Manage vehicle capacity and generate alerts for situations like overcapacity or prolonged stops.
- Reporting and Analytics: Generate statistics and reports on routes, times, passengers, incidents, and resource utilization.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#1A237E) to convey trust, authority, and reliability, reflecting the EMI's institutional nature.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#E8EAF6), offering a clean and professional backdrop that ensures readability and reduces eye strain.
- Accent color: Soft lavender (#D1C4E9) to highlight key interactive elements such as buttons and notifications, adding a touch of modernity and clarity without being jarring.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a grotesque-style sans-serif, will be used for both headlines and body text, for its modern, neutral and machined appearance.
- Note: currently only Google Fonts are supported.
- Use clean and professional icons to represent different modules and actions, ensuring clarity and ease of navigation.
- Maintain a clean and intuitive layout across all screens, prioritizing key information and providing easy access to frequently used features.
- Incorporate subtle animations for transitions and feedback, enhancing user experience without being distracting.
